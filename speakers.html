Himanshu Chakrawarti

CEO, Stellaro Brands

Himanshu Chakrawarti ("Chako/Chakoni"), a Metallurgical Engineer from IIT Kanpur, transitioned from a finance-focused background to a successful marketing career. Starting at Lakmé (HUL) for nine years, he then joined Heinz before a pivotal meeting with Noel Tata in 2000 led him to Westside and the world of retail. Despite a brief hiatus, he returned to retail, driven by its challenges and captivated by its potential. He emphasizes prioritizing the product and customer experience, believing consistent progress, not quick fixes, drives success.

Zabi Khan

Animal Rights Activist, Founder

Zabi, a 25-year-old animal rights activist from India, is a computer science engineer, social entrepreneur, artist, TEDx speaker, humane educator, and changemaker. He runs one of the biggest animal welfare organizations/animal sanctuaries in India, impacting the lives of over 10,000 animals and sensitizing/educating over 133 million people through awareness programs in 12 years. He has received numerous awards, including a Padma Shri nomination, the United Nations Best Volunteer Award 2019, and recognition as the first Ashoka Young Changemaker from Telangana. He participated in MTV Roadies, received the National Youth Icon Award 2018, the Pride of Telangana Award, represented India at the United Nations, and was named India's most influential changemaker in 2021, most inspiring EcoSapien in 2019, and most desirable man in 2022, along with the Rise Young Citizen Award in 2017.
